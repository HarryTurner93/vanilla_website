On Not Burning Out

11th January 2021

This is a short story of my personal experience as a reminder that balance in life is important.

I deliberately called this post On Not Burning Out, because I don't believe I have ever fully burned out. But I have come close several times, and recently it happened again. I am very lucky because it served as a stark reminder that I need to keep things in balance, and it's only now that I have the time to think about and appreciate the lesson, that I realise how important it really is. It's so important, that I'm writing this post as a reminder, mostly for future me, but also for others as well.

I am not a psychologist. I am not a mental health expert. I am not qualified to tell you how to live your life. I'm just one person who's slowly understanding the value of balance, and I hope I can inspire you to do the same.
